About "MoneyWise" magazine

MoneyWise: Short History

Our online newspaper delivers breaking financial news for more than 7 years, both in a printed version and here in our online magazine.

Through its unique visual storytelling, MoneyWise delivers high-quality and engaging content across print, digital, social and video platforms. An innovator of financial news and information, we reflect the pulse of the nation and serve as the host of the American conversation — today, tomorrow and for decades to follow.

Founded in 2019

Our magazine was founded in 2019 by a group of independent journalists specializing in financial, economic and business news. Since the first day, we have been delivering the freshest news from these three spheres to our readers in the US. Quality and easy-to-apprehend presentation of facts quickly made MoneyWise a notable financial magazine in the USA.

Working nationwide since 2019

After a year of our work in New York, we were recognized nationally as #1 financial magazine. It gave us amazing possibilities to develop and grow. We extended our team and invited new journalists to work with us. The number of pages MoneyWise included in January 2019 was 20 but until June it almost doubled.

Digital news since 2019

The information and mobile technologies were developing and that’s why MoneyWise established its online version in 2019. This year became a huge milestone in our history. The same year we developed and launched the beta version of our news resource for mobile phones and other gadgets, which received 500, 000 visitors in the first month.

This year we will be celebrating 6th birthday of our website and our newspaper's 9th birthday.

Today MoneyWise is one of the world’s leading business news organizations, recognized internationally for its authority, integrity, and accuracy. Providing essential news, comment, data and analysis for the global business community, we have a combined paid print and digital circulation in excess of 800,000. Mobile is an increasingly important channel for our magazine, driving more than half of total traffic. MoneyWise provides a broad range of essential services, including news, comment, data, and analysis, to a growing audience of internationally minded professionals.

We cooperate and maintain the constant partnership with other magazines and newspapers worldwide. Our journalists and editors have been sharing their experience with overseas colleagues since 2019 when MoneyWise Journalism partner program started. It made a huge difference in development of global journalism and introduced MoneyWise to other readers all over the world.
